Frequently asked questions

How do I record a note?

Tap the microphone button on the home screen, speak, then tap stop and "Save & Organize." ActionNote transcribes your recording and turns it into a summary, key points, and tasks.

What languages are supported?

Many — ActionNote detects the language you speak (Arabic, English, and more) and organizes your note in that language automatically.

What's included in Pro?

Unlimited notes, "Ask Your Notes" (chat across everything you've captured), iCloud backup, and export. The free plan includes up to 5 notes per month.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the upgrade screen and tap Restore. If you subscribed with the same Apple ID, your Pro access is restored.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: on your iPhone open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → ActionNote, then cancel. You'll keep Pro until the end of the current period.

How is my data handled? Is it private?

Your notes are stored on your device and, if enabled, your personal iCloud — never in a database of ours. To transcribe a recording, your audio is sent securely to our AI (Google Gemini) and isn't kept afterward. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I back up or restore my notes?

Go to Settings → Backup. iCloud backup keeps your notes safe across devices; you can also optionally include audio. Use "Restore from iCloud" on a new device.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and removes your notes from this device and your iCloud.
